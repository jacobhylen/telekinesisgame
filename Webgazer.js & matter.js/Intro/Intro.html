In order to make the gaze tracking work we need to gather some data examples of your eye movement. The data will store locally on your browser and will disappear once you refresh the page.

Make sure you have your webcam turned on, position yourself in good lighting while making sure your face is within the square, keeping it green.

To calibrate the data, move the mouse over the purple appearing dot one by one. Look at the cursor hovering the dot and press on mouse.

Calibration complete.

Continue to collect more data examples if you want to improve the model further, or simply play the game!
